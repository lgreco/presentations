# What is Ungrading?

* A shift: from **scores** to **learning**
* You reflect on and evaluate your own progress
* Writing and self-assessment replace traditional tests
* Grades emerge from honest self-evaluation and dialogue
* The question is not *"What did I score?"* but *"What did I learn?"*
